About Me

I’m a designer and aspiring filmmaker interested in the ways we communicate—through visuals, storytelling, and even subconscious thought. Trained in digital media and design, I specialize in filmmaking for storytelling, graphic design for visual communication, and industrial design for creating functional, innovative products.

Currently in my second year of Global Business and Digital Arts at the University of Waterloo, I explore composition, identity, and narrative through digital and physical media. Proficient in Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and Premiere, I bring both technical skill and a conceptual approach to design.

I’d love to connect and hear how you approach storytelling and visual identity. Let’s talk.
